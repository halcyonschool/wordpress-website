Congratulations to Class of 2019

03.06.2019

Author: Halcyon London International School

On Saturday 25th May, the Halcyon community came together to celebrate and honour the Graduating Class of 2019. The graduation ceremony was a wonderful occasion, supported by an outstanding guest speaker, Dr Alison Powell. Our graduates were marvellous: we enjoyed thoughtful and inspiring speeches from Sam and Beckett, and we were treated to a special musical performance by our G12 band; Mia, Lucy, Maria and Matis. Our warmest congratulations go out to the Class of 2019!

We would like to offer a special thank you to Daniel (G11), our Student Council President, who led the ceremony and presented our speakers. We would also like to thank our staff who, supported and led by Ms. Ranson and Darrien (G10), prepared a very creative teachers' address video. This year, 160 people attended the event - our biggest graduation yet - and we would like to thank everyone for their support. You can enjoy a short video of the event below.

Finally, we would like to wish all our graduates the very best for the future. We look forward to hearing about their new adventures, be that a gap year or the beginning of their studies at different universities around the world. They all deserve their success, and will carry a piece of Halcyon with them as they go out into the world.

We wish you the best of luck!

Our students graduate from Halcyon as part of a community that celebrates every ambition, aspiration and achievement. Empowered to lead, create and collaborate, our graduates are ready to tackle the challenges of a complex future, inspired by a dynamic IB environment that engages every voice in personalised learning opportunities.

Our community is often inspired by the achievements of our graduates: their remarkable successes at elite colleges and universities; their leadership of creative projects in the arts; and the contributions that they bring to our future as collaborative, global citizens.
